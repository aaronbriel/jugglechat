Stock up on supplies, take everyday precautions to keep space and distance between yourself and others, avoid people who are sick, avoid crowds, and wash your hands often. Please limit travel and stay at home as much as possible. If there is an outbreak in your community, stay at home as much as possible and watch for symptoms and emergency signs. If you do get sick, stay home and call your doctor.   To learn more about how to prepare, click here: https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/specific-groups/high-risk-complications.html
