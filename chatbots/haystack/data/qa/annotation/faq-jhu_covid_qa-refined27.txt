Coronavirus are a type of virus - there are many kinds, and some of them can cause disease. Examples of coronaviruses include the common cold, flu, to more severe diseases like Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS-CoV) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS-CoV).
