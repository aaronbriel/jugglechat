At this time, American citizens, lawful permanent residents, and family members (as specified in the Presidential Proclamationexternal icon) who have been in countries with a Level 3 travel health notice within 14 days prior to their arrival will be allowed to enter the United States. Travelers from some countries with a level 3 travel health notice are being re-directed to certain airports and are screened for COVID-19 symptoms such as fever, cough or trouble breathing and asked questions about their travel and possible exposure to COVID-19. Travelers without symptoms will be told to stay home and practice social distancing.. . More information for travelers from countries with a level 3 travel health notice is available here.
