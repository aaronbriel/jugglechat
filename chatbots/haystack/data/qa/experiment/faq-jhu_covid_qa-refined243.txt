The cancellation of events and the closure of schools and kindergartens fall within the responsibility of the Federal Laender and/or the local authorities. In the meantime, all the Federal Laender have cancelled events and closed schools and kindergartens. Only in a very few exceptional cases may an event be allowed.. . The Robert Koch Institute has published “General Principles of Risk Assessment and Recommendations for Action with respect to Large-Scale Events” (PDF-File) to support local authorities in deciding on whether to cancel an event..
